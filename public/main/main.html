welcome

hi, i'm melissa. this space is meant to be a digital scrapbook of sorts for me to express myself and share my thoughts, feelings, and interests without the constraints of mainstream platforms and the pressure of social media. this site is under heavy construction and likely will be for awhile, but feel free to take a look around.

this site was designed to be viewed in firefox with a 1920x1080 display, but should be serviceable in other browsers or resolutions and has basic mobile support. i'm still new to running a site so if anything seems really broken please contact me to let me know! if you're viewing on desktop, the windows to the side are all draggable and collapsible; be sure to say hi in the chatbox to the right before you go!
